About Me

Hi my name is Krish. I grew up in Burlington Township, New Jersey. My passion for computer science began in middle school when my computer class teacher introduced us to code.org. From there on I would often try and familiarize myself with coding and trying to program the little games from code.org. In high school I joined the FIRST robotics team, where I quickly became a core member of the team as a programmer. I programmed the robot's autonomous movements as well as key functions such as moving, shooting balls, and climbing. I found my work on the team to be very fun and decided to pursue computer science as a major. After several projects and internships, I have gained a lot of experience in this field. I am thankful to my mentors that have guided me to where I am but even more so cannot wait to see where my future takes me.
